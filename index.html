About

How I think about
systems and decisions.

My work combines structure, curiosity, and implementation. I'm interested in how data, systems, and AI can be shaped into tools that are technically sound and genuinely useful in real operating environments.

What I care about

I'm drawn to ideas that connect intelligence with action: systems that make work clearer, decisions more grounded, and organizations more capable of doing meaningful things.

How I think

I value simplicity, depth, and relevance. I prefer solutions that are elegant, understandable, and shaped around real human and business needs — not complexity for its own sake.

My approach

I start with the problem, not the technology. Understanding what clarity actually looks like for a team or organization comes before any solution takes shape.

From there, I look for the smallest, most precise intervention that moves things forward — whether that's a model, a workflow, or simply a better framing of the problem itself.
